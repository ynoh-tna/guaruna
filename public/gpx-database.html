Frequently asked questions

How do I find routes in a specific area?

Pan and zoom the map to your area, or search a place. The list shows every route whose track falls inside the current map view. Use the filters for distance, elevation and activity.

Can I add photos or info to a route?

Yes. Open a route and add a photo with your name and a note — the trailhead address, parking, access or any tip. Photos are resized and their EXIF/GPS data is stripped.

What happens to a route I upload?

It becomes public so anyone can view and download it. Please do not upload private or sensitive tracks. Photos are re-encoded and their EXIF/GPS data is stripped.

Can I open a route in the viewer?

Yes. Every route opens in the GPX viewer with its map, elevation profile and splits.
