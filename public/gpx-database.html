Frequently asked questions

Is the GPX route database free?

Yes. Browsing, searching and downloading routes is free, and you can add your own.

What happens to a route I upload?

It becomes public so anyone can view and download it. Please do not upload private or sensitive tracks.

Can I open a route in the viewer?

Yes. Every route opens in the GPX viewer with its map, elevation profile and splits.
