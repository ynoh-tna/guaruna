Pace and speed, both directions

Pace is time per distance (e.g. 5:00 per km); speed is distance per time (e.g. 12 km/h). To go from speed to pace, divide 60 by your speed in km/h. To switch km and miles, multiply or divide by 1.609.

Common conversions

  • 5:00 /km = 8:03 /mile = 12.0 km/h = 7.46 mph
  • 12.0 km/h = 5:00 /km = 7.46 mph
  • 10.0 km/h = 6:00 /km = 6.21 mph

Can I convert pace to speed and back?

How do I convert min/km to min/mile?

Enter your pace in min/km and the converter shows min/mile instantly, along with km/h and mph.
